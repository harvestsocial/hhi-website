Who we are

A movement for young leaders

Generation Next exists to empower teens, students, and young adults to know Jesus deeply, discover their purpose, and lead with courage in every sphere. Through gatherings, mentorship, and discipleship, we champion the next wave of Kingdom builders across campuses, cities, and nations.
