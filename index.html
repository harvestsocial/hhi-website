Harvest House International Church

Harvest House International Church began in 1995 in the home of its founders and Senior Pastors, Bishops Dr. Colin and Sarah Nyathi. What started with just eight people gathered in their lounge has grown into a global movement. Today, HHI has more than 300 churches across the world.

In Bulawayo, Zimbabwe, this vision has taken deep root. Week after week, thousands come together to worship, serve, and grow. We are committed to building leaders, strengthening families, and raising a generation that is passionate about the Gospel.

Our mission is clear: to take the nations for Christ Jesus. We preach the Gospel with signs and wonders following, bringing life and healing to the lost, the wounded, the lonely, the rejected, and the downtrodden.

Bishop Dr. Colin Nyathi

Apostolic leader & visionary of Harvest House International

Bishop Nyathi is a pastor, church planter and author whose ministry has impacted nations. With a passion for revival, leadership development and evangelism, HHI has grown from humble beginnings to a global church family. HHICC—our 10,500-seater auditorium—stands as a testimony to God’s faithfulness and the diligence of the Harvest Family.
