Our Leaders

Who they are and the heart behind their ministry

Who are our leaders?

Bishops Dr. Colin & Sarah Nyathi

Founders & Senior Pastors of Harvest House International

Bishop Dr. Colin Nyathi is an apostolic leader, church planter and author whose ministry has touched many nations. Together with Bishop Dr. Sarah Nyathi, they provide spiritual oversight to the Harvest House International family, mentoring pastors, raising leaders, and carrying a strong mandate for revival, evangelism and discipleship.

Their lives model faith, sacrifice and obedience. Under their leadership, Harvest House International has continued to grow in depth and in reach, always centred on the presence of God, the preaching of the Word and the work of the Holy Spirit.

Journey

Milestones in their call

Moments that shaped their ministry and the Harvest House International family.

1995

Commissioned to plant

Colin and Sarah Nyathi answer the call to pioneer Harvest House, gathering believers hungry for revival in Bulawayo.

2002

First church plants released

Daughter churches are launched, with the Bishops fathering young pastors and setting the pace for multiplication.

2010

Global missions mandate

International outreaches accelerate, taking teams, training, and relief work into new nations across Africa and beyond.

2016

Harvest House International Conference Centre

The 10,500-seater HHICC opens under their leadership, becoming a home for worship, conferences, and training.

2020

Digital discipleship

Online services, prayer streams, and equipping courses expand, keeping the family connected during global disruption.

Today

Raising the next generation

The Bishops continue to mentor emerging leaders, advancing church planting, campus ministry, and humanitarian work.
