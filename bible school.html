Harvest House School of Ministry

Bible School

Equipping leaders to “take the nations for Christ” through sound doctrine, Spirit-led training and practical ministry development.

Founded 1995

Drs. Colin & Sarah Nyathi launched the School of Ministry shortly after starting Harvest House International in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe.

7,000+ Graduates

Since the early 2000s more than seven thousand students have completed programs across 14 nations.

Affiliated & Accredited

The Zimbabwean campus partners with the Ministry of Higher & Tertiary Education and Good Shepherd Ministries (US).

Programs

School of Ministry Tracks

Courses are designed to move students from biblical foundations to leadership in hubs around the world. Many campuses mirror the Bulawayo curriculum so that every student receives the same impartation.

  • ●
    Certificate in Biblical Studies. Core doctrine, hermeneutics, and practical ministry skills.
  • ●
    School of Evangelism. Soul-winning strategies, crusade planning, and discipleship tools used across 716 congregations.
  • ●
    Leadership Intensives. Mentorship with Bishops Colin & Sarah Nyathi focusing on governance, campus planting, and church administration.

Why Bible School?

Harvest House International began with fewer than ten believers meeting in the Nyathi living room. Today, more than 7,000 members worship across 14 countries because leaders were trained and sent.
