Serving Requirements

  • Be born again, committed to HHI and living a Christ-like lifestyle.
  • Attend rehearsals or team briefings on time when scheduled.
  • Serve on assigned Sundays or midweek events with a joyful attitude.
  • Stay accountable to your ministry leader and keep learning.
