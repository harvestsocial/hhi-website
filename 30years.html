30 Years of Harvest

Key moments that shaped Harvest House International into a global family. Scroll through milestones that mark our journey of faith and mission.

1995

Humble Beginnings

Harvest House International starts in a living room with just a handful of believers hungry for God’s presence.

2002

First Church Plant

Our first daughter church is planted, signaling the start of a multiplication movement across the region.

2010

Global Missions

Teams are sent to new nations, launching hubs that bring the Gospel, worship, and community programs abroad.

2016

HHICC Auditorium

The 10,500-seater Harvest House International Conference Centre opens, becoming a beacon for revival gatherings.

2020

Digital Ministry

Online services and live streams expand, connecting thousands every week across continents.
